About Us

We're a small team of carpentry specialists based out of Central & Eastern PEI.

Island roofing and Siding Inc. was created in the winter of 2018. After several years of collaborating on jobs under both Erwin's and Aram's former companies, the Island Roofing and Siding Inc's three collaborated decided to join under a new name.

With over 40 years combined experience, we focus on high-quality craftsmanship on projects as small as a window installation, and as large full residential and commercial exterior builds. While we specialize in roofing and siding, we will take on all types of projects including decks, windows and doors, 5" Seamless Eavestrough, etc.

Our Team

Erwin Coles

Owner & Operator

Erwin has over 40 years of experience working as a professional carpenter. Starting at just 15 years old, he has spent most of his career working with siding around PEI as well as in Calgary.

In 2000 he created Master Exteriors, which he operated until creating Island Roofing and Siding Inc. with his older son, Matt, and their business partner Aram.

Matt Coles

Owner & Operator

Matt has been working in Carpentry since he started working with his father, Erwin as a summer job as a teenager.

After high school Matt got an HVAC Technician diploma from Holland College, but has primarily worked in carpentry over the past 15 years.

Aram Bavampour

Owner & Operator

Aram has worked in the business for over 15 years, specializing in roofing.

He spent several years operating his own business before joining his long-time coworkers Matt and Erwin in creating Island Roofing and Siding.
